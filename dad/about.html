About Us

Building trust through consistency, experience, and service.

More Grace & Blessing Nigeria Enterprise is a well-established Nigerian trading business based in Lagos State. Operating from Potoku Market along the Igando–Ikotun Road, the enterprise has built a strong reputation in the wholesale and retail distribution of plastic products and household items.

Over the years, the business has consistently provided a wide range of plastic souvenirs, kitchen tools, baskets, storage containers, and general merchandise to individuals, retailers, and bulk buyers. Our operations are guided by reliability, product variety, and a strong commitment to customer satisfaction.

With a physical presence in one of Lagos’s active markets, More Grace & Blessing Nigeria Enterprise continues to serve customers with honesty, accessibility, and professionalism. We believe in long-term relationships built on trust and consistent service delivery.

Our Core Focus

  • Wholesale and retail distribution of plastic products
  • Plastic souvenirs and household items
  • Kitchen tools and storage solutions
  • General merchandise trading
  • Customer-focused service
